|**Flag**|**What it Means**|**Why CompTIA Tests It**|
|---|---|---|
|**`-sS`**|**SYN Scan** (Stealth / Half-Open)|Fast and quiet. It doesn't complete the 3-way handshake, making it harder for basic logs to detect.|
|**`-sT`**|**TCP Connect Scan**|Completes the full 3-way handshake. It's loud, easily logged, but highly accurate.|
|**`-sV`**|**Version Detection**|Grabs banner information to tell you the _exact version_ of software running on an open port (crucial for finding known exploits).|
|**`-O`**|**OS Detection**|Attempts to fingerprint and identify the operating system of the target machine.|
